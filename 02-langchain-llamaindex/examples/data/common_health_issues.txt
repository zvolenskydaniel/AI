Issue: Common Cold / Flu

Symptoms:
- Fever or chills
- Runny or blocked nose
- Sore throat
- Cough
- Muscle or body aches
- Fatigue or weakness
- Headache

Description:
The common cold and flu are viral infections affecting the respiratory system.
They spread easily through airborne droplets or contact with contaminated surfaces.
Symptoms usually appear within a few days after exposure and may last from several days to two weeks.

Solution:
- Rest and allow the body to recover
- Drink plenty of fluids to avoid dehydration
- Use over-the-counter medication for symptom relief (e.g. fever or pain)
- Isolate when possible to prevent spreading infection
- Seek medical help if symptoms worsen or persist unusually long

Issue: Cough

Symptoms:
- Dry or productive coughing
- Throat irritation
- Chest discomfort
- Shortness of breath (in severe cases)

Description:
A cough is a reflex action to clear the airways of mucus, irritants, or foreign particles.
It can be caused by infections, allergies, dry air, or environmental irritants.

Solution:
- Stay hydrated to soothe the throat
- Avoid smoke or strong odors
- Use cough lozenges or warm drinks
- Rest the voice and body
- Consult a healthcare professional if cough lasts more than several weeks

==================================================

Issue: Headache

Symptoms:
- Pain or pressure in the head
- Sensitivity to light or sound
- Tight feeling around the forehead or temples
- Fatigue

Description:
Headaches are a common condition that can result from stress, dehydration, lack of sleep,
eye strain, or tension in neck and shoulder muscles.

Solution:
- Rest in a quiet, dark environment
- Drink water to address possible dehydration
- Take regular breaks from screens
- Use relaxation techniques
- Seek medical advice if headaches are frequent or severe

Issue: Minor Burn

Symptoms:
- Red or irritated skin
- Mild swelling
- Pain at the affected area
- Blistering (in some cases)

Description:
Minor burns are caused by brief contact with heat, hot liquids, or steam.
They typically affect only the outer layer of skin and heal within days to weeks.

Solution:
- Cool the burn under running cool water
- Keep the area clean and protected
- Avoid breaking blisters
- Cover loosely if needed
- Seek medical help if the burn is large or shows signs of infection

==================================================

Issue: Upset Stomach

Symptoms:
- Nausea
- Abdominal discomfort
- Bloating
- Mild diarrhea
- Loss of appetite

Description:
An upset stomach can be caused by dietary issues, stress, mild infections,
or intolerance to certain foods.

Solution:
- Eat light and bland foods
- Drink fluids in small amounts
- Avoid heavy or spicy meals
- Rest until symptoms improve
- Consult a professional if symptoms persist or worsen
